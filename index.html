About

I’ve been in a committed relationship with Python for over 5 years now — and honestly, it’s going great. Together we’ve built web apps, wrangled data pipelines, tamed GUIs, and occasionally broken production (for educational purposes, of course). I’ve coded solo and in teams, surviving merge conflicts and mysterious bugs that only appear when your boss is watching. I’ve dabbled in everything from Django to PySide6, tinkered with NumPy and Pandas like a mad scientist, and even pretended to understand Airflow’s error logs. My toolkit includes automation scripts, web scrapers, dashboards that probably deserve design awards, and a few “temporary” hacks that somehow went to production. In short: I speak fluent Python, think in for-loops, and can debug faster than you can say “it works on my machine.”
